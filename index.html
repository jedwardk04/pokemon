These are my favorite Pokemon

In no particular order

  1. Dragonite
  2. Obstagoon
  3. Buzzwole

Dragonite

This Gen1 Pokemon is amongst the fan favorites for many reasons. The first being that it is the only sole dragon type from generatoin 1, and is considered a pseudo-legendary

Gen 1 refers to the first generation of pokemon released aka R/B/Y

Obstagoon

Obstagoon is a divergent evolution of the Galarian Zigzagoon. In this relative to the normal type Zigzagoon line, it gains a dark typing, which is very useful. And also looks incredibly punk, goth, and metal at the same time.

Buzzwole

What's not to love about a bug/fighting type Pokemon, that looks like an alien verison of prime Arnold Schwarzenegger? Buzzwole is in a class of Pokemon called the Ultra Beasts. These handful of powerful legendary Pokemon entered our world through a wormhole, and defy many normal laws of Pokemon structure.

I have not played this generation of games, but have encountered and adored the Ultra Beasts in Pokemon Go
